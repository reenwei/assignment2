Verification


Here at Climb Central, we also offer verification courses for the following:
1. Top Rope
2. Lead


Do note that:

  • Top Rope Verification will only be conducted at Novena
  • Lead Verification will be conducted at either Sports Hub or Funan
  • Bookings will open 3 days in advance at 12 noon. All bookings for Verification should be done in pairs
  • These slots are meant for the purpose of verification only. If you wish to continue climbing after completing your verification, you are required to make a separate booking for a climbing slot at the respective outlet
  • Ad-hoc verifications are suspended until further notice
  • For more information, the FAQs on Verification can be found here

Top Rope Verification

After clearing this verifiication, it verifies that you can belay on the top rope walls using a belay device attached to your harness. Verification must be taken on an assisted braking device (ABD) and you will need to bring your own belay device as we only have the Edelrid MegaJul for rental.
If you require more information about ABDs, click here.

Pre-requisites:
Attend a Top Rope class OR at least 1 year of climbing and belaying experience.
Do note that the minimum age to take the verification and velay is 13 and above.

Top Rope Verification (Novena)
Cost: $10 verification fee per pax for each verification

Lead Verification

After clearing this verifiication, it verifies that you can lead climb and lead belay. Verification must be taken on an assisted braking device (ABD) as you are required to use an ABD on our lead walls.
You will need to bring your own belay device as we only have the Edelrid MegaJul for rental.
If you require more information about ABDs, click here.

Pre-requisites:
Attend a Lead class OR at least 3 years of Lead climbing and belaying experience.
Do note that the minimum age to take the verification and velay is 13 and above.
