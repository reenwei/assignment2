Welcome to climb central!

Here at climb central, no prior climbing experience is required to start climbing! Start your climbing adventures from your first visit, with our easy-to-learn safety systems and 15-minute safety briefing conducted by our staff, who are experienced individuals and have gone through the relevant verfication courses.

Come on down and experience a unique experience for yourself, whether it be on the

  • 4 Meter Boulder Wall
  • Top Rope (Up to 16 Meters)
  • Lead Climbing (Up to 16 Meters)
  • Auto-Belays (Up to 16 Meters)
  • Or All of the above!

If you are worried about the weather, fret not! Climb Central is completely indoors, whether rain or shine you can climb all day in air-conditioned comfort.

The options are limitless and the choice is yours! So what are you waiting for, head on down to any of our outlets today :)
