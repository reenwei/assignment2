First time climbing?

Don't know what to do? That's fine! We all have to start somewhere.
Our entry requirements are as follows:
1. Age 5+
2. Weight more than 20kg
3. Taller than 1.1m
Additionally, all children aged 5 to 12+ are required to have a responsible adult aged 18+ accompanying them into the facility.
The responsible adult must actively supervise the children while within the facility and each adult can supervise up to a maximum of 2 children
Kindly note that entry rates still apply!
